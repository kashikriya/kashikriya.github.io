Home - Our Sweet little Yoga Studio in beautiful Gisborne

Located in peaceful suburbs of Gisborne is a special Yoga Studio run by Yogi Prakash Shankar Vyas. Lovingly known as 'Guruji' (Guru-jee)  by his students.
He teaches Hatha Yoga to all levels in a style that is unique to him. Guruji's students from around the world share remarkable stories of positive and profound change in their lives, having learned yoga from him.

Kashi Kriya Yoga Centre Aim to promote:

  • Regular practice of yoga to increases self-awareness and concentration.
  • Peace of mind, good health, and love and respect for nature.
  • An environment for physical and spiritual development.

About Guruji - All about our Guruji

Guruji is a 'Householder Yogi': a spiritual master who teaches Hatha Yoga for a living and has a family. He does not have an ashram, nor does he run a business or an organisation. He is an authorised master of Kriya Yoga in the direct lineage of the great Yogi Sri Lahiri Mahasaya, whose name became known worldwide through the publication of Autobiography of a Yogi by Paramahansa Yogananda.

And if you saw Guruji tending to his plants and vegetables in the back garden, one of his favourite pastimes, you could be excused for imagining that he is an ordinary family man. Because there are no external signs to make you think twice, if you passed Guruji at the local market or on the street, you would never guess that many devotees and disciples around the world recognise in him something rare and wonderful, and go to extraordinary lengths to meet him, ask his advice, hear him speak and just to be close to him.

Yoga Classes - Ahh! The good stuff

Yoga Classes are held in our peaceful studios.
Hatha Yoga classes are typically comprised of three parts:

  1. Hatha Yoga (physical exercises)
  2. Pranayama (breathing exercises)
  3. Raja Yoga (practice of Pranayama and meditation)
Classes are of one-hour duration and are taught in small groups. Please see class details for more information. Sometimes after the yoga class, there is an opportunity to listen to explanations on yoga and spirituality, and to ask Guruji questions.

  • Thursday 10.00-11.00
  • Saturday 10.00-11.00
Additional Information: For your own comfort please wear loose and comfortable clothing. It is advisable not to eat three to four hours before and half an hour after a yoga class.

Kriya Yoga Seekers - Welcome to authentic practice

Kriya Yoga is a path to self realisation. Guruji teaches the original practice which was passed to him by his Guru - the grandson of Sri Lahiri Mahasaya. If you feel strongly drawn to this path please email us at seekers@kashikriyavolunteers.com with your introduction and why you feel drawn to apply for Kriya Yoga initiation.
